How selection works

A simple three-step process

01

Document review

The lab reviews your background, technical preparation, and the clarity of your motivation for joining.

02

Discussion and refinement

Interview sessions are used to sharpen your proposed direction and test whether the research fit is real.

03

Topic alignment
