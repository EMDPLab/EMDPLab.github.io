About EMDP Lab

EMDP Lab is a dedicated research group focused on exploring materials engineering and chemistry, as well as developing control strategies to improve the efficiency of electronic devices. We investigate novel functionalities driven by new fabrication strategies and material combinations.

We are looking for highly motivated Master/PhD students to join us. If you are interested and have much fun in digging, please email Prof. Dong Hae Ho (hodh123@dgist.ac.kr).
